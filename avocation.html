In my free time, one of my favorite things is spending quality time with my family. Our favorite game to play together is cards. I also enjoy a variety of activities. From sports, I like competitive games like bowling and swimming, which I do once a month or during my summer break. Additionally, I love sitting back and watching a good basketball game from time to time.

Playing Cards

Playing cards is one of the best games to enjoy with family. It's a great way to chat and bond while having fun.

Bowling

Bowling is perfect for playing with friends and siblings. It's a competitive and engaging game. There are places in Addis where you can play, and it's a lot of fun.

Swimming

Initially, I disliked swimming and was afraid of it, but my father coached me well, and now I enjoy it. For those who hate or fear it, I encourage you to face your fears and enjoy the experience.

Watching Volleyball

I recently started watching volleyball, and it's a great way to relax. I'm a fan of the Japanese team—they have excellent teamwork and made history by finishing second place last year.
